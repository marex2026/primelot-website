How It Works

A Proven Five-Step
Liquidation Process

Every Prime Lot engagement follows a structured, transparent workflow — from first conversation to final settlement — designed to reduce your risk and maximize your recovery.

1

Inventory Your Assets

A meticulous inventory — descriptions, photographs, condition notes, and documentation — establishing a complete picture of what you have and what it's worth.

2

Explore Your Options

We guide selection of the best disposition method — auctions, direct sales, private treaty — considering asset condition, market trends, legal requirements, and timeline.

3

Determine Liquidation Value

Using market analysis and professional appraisal, we establish realistic recovery values — accounting for market conditions, buyer motivation, and seller urgency.

4

Develop a Strategic Plan

We collaborate with your team and legal partners to design a clear, actionable plan — cost assessments, timelines, compliance steps — minimizing disruption while maximizing returns.

5

Execute & Settle

Our specialists execute the plan with precision, leveraging multi-channel buyer networks to achieve optimal results, followed by transparent settlement and court-ready reporting.
